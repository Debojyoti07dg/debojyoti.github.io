Who I am

About Me

Researcher, teacher, and builder — driven by the belief that AI should serve humanity and the planet.

My research journey has been driven by a desire to use Artificial Intelligence to create meaningful real-world impact. I am a PhD researcher at IIT Kharagpur, with my thesis submitted in February 2026, working at the intersection of AI, Computer Vision, and Sustainability.

A major part of my work focuses on intelligent waste classification, where I develop models that can accurately recognise waste to support efficient recycling and cleaner urban environments. The potential of AI to contribute to environmental sustainability continues to inspire my work.

With an academic background in mathematics and computer science, I enjoy connecting theoretical ideas with practical applications across waste management, supply chain optimisation, and demand forecasting.

I have published research in reputed journals and explored innovative models that combine deep learning with human insight. My teaching experience at IIT Kharagpur and NPTEL has strengthened my mentorship and collaboration skills.

I aspire to push the boundaries of AI to create technologies that are not only intelligent but also responsible, sustainable, and socially beneficial.
